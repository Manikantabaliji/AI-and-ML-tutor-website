Data Science

Data science is a multidisciplinary field that involves extracting insights and knowledge from data. It combines elements from various disciplines, including statistics, computer science, mathematics, and domain expertise, to analyze and interpret complex data sets. The main goal of data science is to turn raw data into actionable insights, predictions, and recommendations
